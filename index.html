When & Where

Date: Saturday 13th of May, 2023.

Celebration: Pieve di Sant’Andrea di Bigonzo, Vittorio Veneto (TV), 15:00.
Map, Info

Reception: Garden “al Brolo” at the restaurant “da Gigetto”, Miane (TV).
Map, Website

Travel

Car: you have to arrive here!

Train: Vittorio Veneto’s station is located on the railway line connecting Venezia and Belluno. You can conveniently plan your trip at Trenitalia’s website.

Plane: the closest airports are Treviso and Venezia. If you are not planning to rent a car (which is probably the best option), the most convenient solution is probably to get, respectively, to Treviso’s and Venezia Mestre’s train stations and get a train there. At these links more info on transports for Treviso and Venezia.

Please let us know if you have any issues and we will try to sort them out!
(e.g. you do not have any means of transport from the church to the reception)

Accommodation

There are several B&Bs in Vittorio Veneto and surroundings. If you want to be closer to the reception’s venue, we suggest to stay in Miane or Combai. Some alternatives are B&B Ernestina, B&B Isabella, B&B Casa di Campagna. Let them know you are there for our wedding upon contacting them.

Once again, please let us know if you have any issues and we will try to sort them out!
(e.g. you want to share a big apartment with other guests)

What to see & do

Vittorio Veneto: this is our hometown! Here and here you can find some turistic info.

Surroundings: the “Colline del Prosecco” area has recently been added to the list of UNESCO world heritage sites. This webpage contains a lot of useful turistic information about the municipalities included.

More distant but worth the visit: Treviso and, of course, Venice and the Dolomites.


This is clearly a non-exhaustive list, so do not hesitate to ask us for more tips!
