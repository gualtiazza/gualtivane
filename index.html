Car: you have to arrive here!

What to see & do

Vittorio Veneto: this is our hometown! Here and here you can find some turistic info.


Recommended activities: walk around Serravalle, hike to Santa Augusta’s sanctuary, enjoy typical food at Trattoria alla Cerva or at Hostaria Via Caprera, drink aperitivo and eat delicius pastries at Caff√® Commercio.

Surroundings: the “Colline del Prosecco” area has recently been added to the list of UNESCO world heritage sites. This webpage contains a lot of useful turistic information about the municipalities included.

Recommended activities: visit the villages of Cison di Valmarino and Follina, taste Prosecco at Bortolin Angelo’s winery, try the famous Osteria Senz’Oste.
